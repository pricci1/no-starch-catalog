If your rubber band is too long, loop it around the
farthest towball pin (u) or around both towball pins
(u and v) at the same time. you want enough ten-
sion in your rubber band to pull the Trigger back
through the safety catch mechanism (in the slide)
after the slide has moved forward again. after you’ve
inished the model, you may need to come back
to this step and do some experimenting to ind the
optimal amount of tension.

Make sure the rubber band its between the 12-stud
Technic brick and the 1/2-stud bushing as shown
at w—this will stop interference between this rubber
band and the one that powers the slide.
