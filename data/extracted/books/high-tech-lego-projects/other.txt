         building the pointer
    The LEGO Satellite Spotter uses two motors: an EV3 Large
    Motor to point toward the azimuth, which can be anywhere
    from 0 to 360 degrees, and an EV3 Medium Motor to point
    toward the elevation, which can be anywhere from 0 to
    90 degrees. The EV3 Medium Motor rides on the EV3 Large
    Motor, as shown in Figure 4.
         Use the following building instructions to build the
    motorized pointing arm. The mount for the dGPS is the same
    as the one used in the LEGO Car Tracker bonus project,
    moved to the other side of the EV3 Intelligent Brick.
