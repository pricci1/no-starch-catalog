Additional Parts Needed with Education Edition
                                                                                          Nov. 20, 2014 Yoshihito Isogawa


The LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3 Idea Book allows you to build all models in this book if you have a set of the LEGO
MINDSTORMS EV3 Home Edition (#31313). Meanwhile, if you have the Education Edition (Core Set #45544 and Expansion
Set #45560), please refer to this list and check which models you can build with your EV3. It assumes that the same shape
parts are the same even if their colors are different.
√ ： You can build it     × ： Additional parts required or a little ingenuity needed

をお持ちの方が、どのモデルを作ることができるかを調べたものです。もちろん、同じ形であれば色が違っていても同じ部品と見なし
ます。
