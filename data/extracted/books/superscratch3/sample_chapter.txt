                       Tip: When setting the rules for winning and losing in your
                       games, use the greater-than symbol (>) or the less-than
                       symbol (<) instead of the equal sign (=) , as we do in

                       breaking when a variable changes too quickly!

                       might touch the Virus a few times in quick succession, and the
                       program won’t realize that you’ve won the game.
