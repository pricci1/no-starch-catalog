ACADEMIC OR REVIEW COPY REQUESTS

We’re pleased to consider your request for review copies or to supply desk copies where your bookstore has ordered new copies.

Examination/review copies:
Examination copies are available to teachers, college professors or instructors considering one of our titles for classroom use or course adoption. Please note we only send ebooks for review.

Please email [email protected] with the course name and number, the term or semester the course will be taught and the anticipated enrollment size.

Desk copies:
We’re pleased to provide complimentary desk copies of any adopted text to instructors at accredited colleges and universities. One desk copy is available for every twenty new copies ordered by your bookstore from a distributor.
To request a desk copy of one of our titles, please email [email protected] with an invoice or a copy of the PO for the requested desk copy.
